About

I have been organizing conferences for the past five years. What I enjoy most is the combination of content and people: shaping a strong topic, bringing together people who care about it, and creating an environment where conversations happen naturally.

A good conference is not just a venue and a timetable. It needs a topic worth discussing, the right speakers, the right audience, and details that are handled properly. When those things come together, the event feels focused, useful, and worth attending.
