About the Conference

A focused gathering for Europe’s class-action community

Business of Class Actions Europe brings together the people behind collective litigation across Europe to discuss how collective actions work in practice — from building and funding cases to certification, settlement and distribution.

Designed as a practical, business-oriented forum, the conference focuses on the questions that matter: what makes collective actions succeed, where they fail and how different stakeholders are shaping the market across Europe.

Hosted at World of Wine in Porto, the event combines a focused programme with meaningful networking and one of Europe’s most active collective redress jurisdictions.
