Why Porto

The right city for this conversation

Porto is not a coincidence. It is a deliberate choice — and not only because World of Wine is one of the best conference venues in southern Europe.

  • Collective redress relevance Portugal has actively transposed the RAD Directive and is attracting growing attention as a jurisdiction for European consumer and competition claims. This is a working location, not a backdrop.
  • Compact and easy to navigate Porto is small enough that the conference doesn’t fragment. Delegates stay close, eat at the same restaurants and continue the conversation. The city does what a good conference venue should.
  • Direct connections across Europe Porto Airport serves direct flights from London, Amsterdam, Brussels, Frankfurt, Paris, Madrid and a dozen other European hubs. Most delegates arrive in under three hours.
  • February is the right time Before the spring conference season. Quiet, affordable, unhurried. Hotels and restaurants are available without the summer premium and without the crowds.
