Conference location

World of Wine

Perched on the south bank of the Douro River in Vila Nova de Gaia, World of Wine (WOW) is one of Porto’s most striking cultural districts, with sweeping views across the river to the historic Ribeira and the Luís I Bridge. Opened in 2021 within beautifully converted port-wine cellars, the 55,000 m² complex brings together seven museums, a dozen restaurants and bars, a wine school and dedicated event spaces — a fitting setting for two days of class-action discussion. It is roughly 25 minutes by car from the airport.

Rua do Choupelo 39, 4400-088 Vila Nova de Gaia, Portugal
